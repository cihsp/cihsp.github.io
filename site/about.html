Can't I Have Some Privacy?

What is C I H S P?

Can't I Have Some Privacy is a final project for COMP 426 created by James Allen and James Martin.

Our goal was to create a mobile-friendly Yelp!-styled guide to bathrooms on UNC's campus!

Reviews are crowdsourced, bathrooms currently are not. If you would like to submit a bathroom, please send us mail via the address in the footer!
